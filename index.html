Who Am I ?

About Me

I'm a passionate and versatile Software Developer, Database Administrator, and Data Analyst with two decades of hands-on experience on various fields building and optimizing digital solutions across web, desktop, and data platforms. I specialize in backend development, database architecture, and data-driven decision-making, with a strong command of technologies like JavaScript, SQL Server, MySQL, Python and Django, crafting powerful desktop solutions with VB.NET, C#, and leveraging R for advanced data analysis and pipeline automation.
Throughout my career, I've led the development of enterprise-grade applications, streamlined data systems, and driven digital transformation across academic institutions and private enterprises. From designing retail stock management systems to migrating decades of financial data, I thrive on solving complex problems and delivering scalable, user-friendly solutions.
Beyond the code, I’m a dedicated mentor and trainer, committed to empowering teams and enhancing productivity through knowledge sharing. I’m always exploring new technologies—from AI tools like Microsoft Copilot Studio to cloud-based platforms like SAP—to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving tech landscape.
When I’m not immersed in development or data, you’ll find me cycling, gaming, or diving into a good book. Let’s connect and build something impactful together.
